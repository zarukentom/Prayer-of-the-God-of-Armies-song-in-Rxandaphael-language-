# Prayer-of-the-God-of-Armies-song-in-Rxandaphael-language-

 As you listen to the music video below, may God bless you.

1) Rxandaphael language: Etkal ne Zumkarim, Etkal ne Etkalim, Christos Yesua, Or-Zulielum Varielum-ah! Tona et Blen Thonir ne Varielum. Amenielum.

1) English translation: God of Armies, God of gods, Christ Jesus, be praised forever! You are the greatest spiritual being of all time. Amen 

2) Rxandaphael language: Elivc Elivc Elivc et Let Etkal Blenvar.
Na-aed, Se-aed, en Or-aed, Lumara Let en Etkal.
Tona et Yivte te Zenielum Lumshariel en Blen.
Etl Tona na-zlad Elelen, en el Yivte ne Tona, Varnum na-deilum Aed en Ashlum.
Elivc Elivc Elivc et Let, Lumara Let Yesua Christos, Etkal ne Zumkarim.

2) English translation: Holy Holy Holy is the Lord God almighty. 
Who was, who is, and who is to come, our Lord and God. You are worthy to receive glory honor and power. For you created all things and by your will, they were given existence and life.
Holy Holy Holy is the Lord, our Lord Jesus Christ, The God of Armies. 

3) Rxandaphael language: Tona et Yivte te Thonak Zumal en te Crumthal ne Lumkariel. 

3) English translation: You are worthy to take the scroll and break open it's seals.

 4) Rxandaphael language: Etl Tona na-kumel en el Tona ne Thonariel Shonar. 

4) English Translation: For you were killed and by your sacrificial death.

5) Rxandaphael language: Tona na-blen Thonai te Etkal el elnir Thona, Zinkael, Nativ, en Thona. 

5) English Translation: You purchase people for God from every tribe, language, nation and race. 

6) Rxandaphael language: Tona na-zlad Varnum Varlum ne Etnivim te Teniv Lumara Etkal.

6) English translation: You made them a kingdom of priest to serve our God.

 7) Rxandaphael language: En Varnum Or-Let el Eld. 

7) English Translation: And they shall reign on earth.

8) Rxandaphael language: Dei Lumal na-kumel 

8) English Translation: The divine lamb that was slain.

 9) Rxandaphael language: Et Yivte te Dei Blen, Varlim, Endl en Blen, Honor, Lumshariel en Zula. 

9) English Translation: Is worthy to receive power, wealth, wisdom and strength, honor, glory and praise.

 10) Rxandaphael language: Te Thonir se-aed el Thonkar, en te Dei Lumal: 

10) English Translation: To Him who sits on the throne, and to the divine lamb:

 11) Rxandaphael language: Or-Zula en Honor, Lumshariel en Kal, Varielum-ah Variel. Amenielum. 

11) English Translation: Be praise and honor, Glory and might, forever and ever.
Amen.

12) Rxandaphael language: Lumara Se-zula Tona Etkal-ah! ne Tona Blen et Blen.

12) English Translation: We honor you oh God! Your power is so great.
